Executive Summary

Dachshund Coin (DACH) represents a paradigm shift from traditional meme tokens to a meaningful, utility-driven cryptocurrency ecosystem. Built on Ethereum with deflationary tokenomics and community governance, DACH aims to create sustainable value for holders while supporting charitable causes and animal welfare initiatives.

Our innovative approach combines automatic token burning, holder rewards, and decentralized governance to create a self-sustaining ecosystem that benefits all participants while maintaining transparency and community ownership.

Key Features

Deflationary Mechanism

2% of every transaction is permanently burned, reducing total supply and creating scarcity over time.

Holder Rewards

2% redistribution to all holders proportionally, creating passive income for long-term investors.

DAO Governance

Community-driven decision making through decentralized governance and transparent voting.

Charity Integration

Built-in charitable giving mechanisms supporting animal welfare and community causes.

Technical Specifications

Smart Contract Details

  • Blockchain: Ethereum (ERC-20)
  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000,000 DACH
  • Decimals: 18
  • Burn Rate: 2% per transaction
  • Redistribution: 2% to holders

Security Features

  • Audit: Third-party security audit
  • Ownership: Renounced contract
  • Liquidity: Locked for 2 years
  • Anti-Whale: Max transaction limits
  • Anti-Bot: Launch protection
